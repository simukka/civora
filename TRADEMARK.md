# Civora Trademark and Brand Policy

Civora's source code and many of its assets are free and open (see
[COPYRIGHT.md](COPYRIGHT.md)). The **Civora name and logo are not**. This policy
explains how you may and may not use them.

SPDX identifier for brand assets: `LicenseRef-Civora-Trademark`
(full text: [LICENSES/LicenseRef-Civora-Trademark.txt](LICENSES/LicenseRef-Civora-Trademark.txt)).

## What this covers

- The name **"Civora"** and the Civora wordmark.
- The Civora **logo**, including `civora_logo.svg`, `logo_concept.png`, and any
  file under `assets/logo/`.
- The names of official realms and components used as brands, such as
  **"Genesis Realm"** and **"Reality Kernel"**, when used as identifiers of this
  project rather than as generic descriptive terms.

These marks are owned by the Civora Authors. The open-source licenses used in
this repository (AGPL-3.0-or-later, Apache-2.0, CC-BY-SA-4.0, CC-BY-4.0) grant
rights to code and content **but do not grant any trademark rights**.

## Why we protect the brand

Civora is designed to be forked. Anyone can run, modify, and redistribute the
software. The trademark exists for one reason: so players can tell **official
Civora** apart from a fork or an impersonation. Protecting the mark protects
users — it does not restrict the freedom of the code.

## Uses that are always fine (no permission needed)

- **Nominative / referential use.** Saying your project is "compatible with
  Civora", "based on Civora", or "a fork of Civora", as long as it is truthful
  and does not imply official endorsement.
- **Discussion, education, journalism, and commentary** about Civora.
- **Redistributing official, unmodified builds** together with the unmodified
  official logo.
- **Community use** such as fan art, meetups, and tutorials, provided you do not
  imply you speak for or are endorsed by the Civora project.

## Uses that need our written permission

- Using the Civora name or logo as the **primary branding** of a product,
  service, fork, server network, token, or company.
- Any use that **implies official status, endorsement, affiliation, or
  sponsorship**.
- **Modifying the logo** (recoloring, redrawing, combining with other marks) and
  still calling it the Civora logo.
- Using the marks on **merchandise for sale**.
- Registering the Civora name (or a confusingly similar name) as a **trademark,
  company name, or domain** intended to trade on Civora's reputation.

## Rules for forks

You may fork the code under its license (for the core, AGPL-3.0-or-later). If
you distribute a fork:

1. **Rebrand if you diverge or could cause confusion.** Give a materially
   modified fork its own name and logo.
2. **Remove or replace the Civora brand assets** unless you have permission to
   keep them, so users are not misled about the source of the software.
3. You may still state, factually, that your project is derived from or
   compatible with Civora.

This mirrors the approach used by many open projects: free code, protected name.

## Requesting permission

For uses that need permission, or if you are unsure, open an issue at
<https://github.com/simukka/civora> or contact the maintainers listed there. We
aim to say yes to good-faith community use.

  governs.
- This document is a policy summary, not legal advice, and does not by itself
